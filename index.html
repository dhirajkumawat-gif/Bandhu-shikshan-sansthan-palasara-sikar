Welcome to Bandhu School Palasara

Shiksha ke kshetra me 22 saal se bhi adhik ke anubhav ke sath, hamara school bacho ko behtareen shiksha aur sanskar dene ke liye pratibadh hai. Hum sirf padhai par hi nahi, balki bacho ke mansik aur sharirik vikas par bhi poora dhyan dete hain.

In pichle 22 saalon me humne hazaron vidyarthiyon ke bhavishya ko nikaara hai. Yahan ka mahool shant aur safe hai, jahan experienced teachers har ek bacche par vyaktigat (personal) dhyan dete hain.
